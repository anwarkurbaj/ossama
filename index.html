1️⃣ Present Simple الحاضر البسيط

يُستخدم للروتين اليومي والحقائق العلمية
Subject + V1 (+ s/es for he/she/it)
alwaysusuallyoften sometimesneverrarely seldomevery dayonce / twice
✅ They play tennis every day.
✅ He usually rings at this time.
✅ The sun rises in the east. (حقيقة)

2️⃣ Past Simple الماضي البسيط

يُستخدم لحدث وقع وانتهى في وقت محدد في الماضي
Subject + V2
yesterdaylast week/month agoin 2005from...to
✅ She moved to Damascus last year.

3️⃣ Present Continuous الحاضر المستمر

يُستخدم للحدث الذي يحدث الآن (لحظة الكلام)
is / am / are + V-ing
nowat the momenttoday nowadaysLook!Listen!
✅ Be quiet! Someone is studying.

4️⃣ Past Continuous الماضي المستمر

يُستخدم لحدثين متقاطعين في الماضي (حدث كان مستمراً وقاطعه حدث آخر)
was / were + V-ing
whenwhileas
✅ While she was driving to school, she had an accident.

5️⃣ Present Perfect Simple الحاضر التام

يُستخدم للحدث الذي وقع في وقت غير محدد وله أثر في الحاضر
have / has + V3
justalreadyyet everneverso far in recent years
✅ I have just finished my work.
✅ Have you ever played tennis?
For (فترة زمنية)Since (وقت محدد)

6️⃣ Present Perfect Continuous الحاضر التام المستمر

يُستخدم للحدث الذي وقع وانتهى لكن تركَ آثاراً واضحة
have / has + been + V-ing
tiredred (face)soft (hands) all day/nightforsince
✅ I have been studying all day. I'm tired.

7️⃣ Past Perfect Simple الماضي التام

يُستخدم لحدثين في الماضي — الأول يأخذ Had+V3 والثاني V2
had + V3
beforeafterwhen by the timeuntilalthough
✅ He had finished his work before he went to bed.

8️⃣ Passive Voice المبني للمجهول

القاعدة الأساسية:
Verb to be + V3
TensePassive Form

✅ The dishes are washed every day by Ruba.

9️⃣ Causative — Having things done السببية

عندما تحصل على خدمة من شخص آخر (لا تفعلها بنفسك)
have / has / had / will have + noun + V3
OriginalCausative
I repair the car.I have the car repaired.
He repairs the car.He has the car repaired.
He repaired the car.He had the car repaired.
I will repair the car.I will have the car repaired.
✅ My father doesn't wash the car himself. → My father has the car washed.

1️⃣4️⃣ Inversion التحويل

عند بدء الجملة بـ (rarely, never, seldom, hardly, no sooner) يُقلب الفعل المساعد قبل الفاعل
✅ I seldom go by bus. → Seldom do I go by bus.
✅ Never have I been to another country.
✅ Rarely had John visited Palmyra.
